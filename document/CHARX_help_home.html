Welcome to the online help for CHARX products by Phoenix Contact

Below is a list of tips for navigating our online documentation.

Viewing topics

  • In the desktop view, all topics (when no filters are applied) can be accessed from the "Contents" tab.
  • In the mobile view, click the "Show left panel" button  to view the content.

Button functions

"Set filters" button — used to filter topics. Click and select all the applicable options.

"Add to favourites" button — used to add a topic to a favourite list.

"Search..." button — used to search keywords within topics. Click and type in the desired keyword.
 

"Show favourites list" button — used to view your favourite topics, for example, when re-visiting this online documentation. For this feature a cookie will be placed on your device but stays strictly anonymous.
